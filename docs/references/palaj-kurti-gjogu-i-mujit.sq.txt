Gjogu i Mujit
=============
The complete Albanian text of the kreshnik song (= Elsie & Mathie-Heck's
"Mujo's Courser", Songs of the Frontier Warriors no. 8). 412 verse lines;
the English translation is line-aligned 1:1 (spot-checked at lines 1, 98-101,
300-301, 405-412 — holds throughout).

Source: recorded in Shala (District of Shkodra). Published in: Visaret e
Kombit, vol. II, ed. Bernardin Palaj and Donat Kurti (Tirana 1937), pp. 70-80;
and Folklor shqiptar II, Epika legjendare (Cikli i kreshnikëve), vol. 1, ed.
Qemal Haxhihasani (Tirana 1966), pp. 109-118. Folk text, public domain.

Extracted 2026-07-15 from the embedded text layer (NOT OCR) of the Albanian
PDF Robert Elsie published alongside his translation:
web.archive.org/web/2023id_/http://www.albanianliterature.net/oralverse/verse_09_AL/verse_09_AL_08.pdf
Verbatim as printed (Gheg orthography of the edition: â, ki', kenka, kenkan,
tuj, m'i, po m'jau ep, s'po...), including oddities kept as printed ("kn thanë"
for "ka thanë" at l.250, "krajlj" at l.137, "giet"/"gia" for "kërkonte"/"gjë",
"kn" for "ka"). Leading margin line-numbers (5, 10, 15...) removed; nothing
else touched. Lines numbered 1-412 below for the beat-file's ¶.sentence refs.
